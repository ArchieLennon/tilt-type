Wonk Display - interactive web specimen.

Please tap to begin, then tilt in any direction to interact with the type. This page uses a mobile phone's tilt and compass hardware to vary aspects of the type.

  • tap the text box to edit the text and sample the typface
  • tilt the phone up and down to manipulate the Y axis
  • tilt the phone left and right to manipulate the X axis

Wonk designed and devloped by Archie Lennon 2022.
